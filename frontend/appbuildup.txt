Backend APIs Complete:-

POST   /api/campaigns/                    — Campaign banao
GET    /api/campaigns/                    — Saare campaigns
GET    /api/campaigns/{id}                — Ek campaign
PUT    /api/campaigns/{id}                — Update
DELETE /api/campaigns/{id}                — Delete
GET    /api/campaigns/{id}/detail         — Full detail with stats
POST   /api/campaigns/{id}/platforms      — Platforms add karo
GET    /api/campaigns/{id}/stats          — Platform stats
POST   /api/campaigns/{id}/ad-content     — Ad content save
GET    /api/campaigns/{id}/ad-content     — Ad content dekho
PUT    /api/campaigns/{id}/ad-content/{id} — Update ad content
POST   /api/leads/                        — Lead add karo
GET    /api/leads/                        — Saare leads
GET    /api/leads/campaign/{id}           — Campaign ke leads
PUT    /api/leads/{id}                    — Status update
DELETE /api/leads/{id}                    — Delete lead
